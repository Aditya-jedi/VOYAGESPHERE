Botswana

Botswana is one of Africa's most popular and exclusive safari destinations. It is a fascinating world, home to well-known landmarks such as the Okavango Delta, the Kalahari Desert and the Chobe National Park.

South Africa

South Africa, the southernmost country on the African continent, renowned for its varied topography, great natural beauty, and cultural diversity, all of which have made the country a favored destination for travelers.

Mauritius

Mauritius is one of the most prosperous countries in Africa.It is known for its beaches, lagoons and reefs.Mauritius is the most densely populated country in Africa due to its small size.It is famous for being the home of the extinct flightless bird,the dodo,which has become a symbol of the island's biodiversity.

Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in southern Africa known for its dramatic landscape and diverse wildlife,much of it within parks,reserves and safari areas.Zimbabwe is home to the world's largest waterfall-Victoria Falls,known locally as 'Mosioa-Tunya'(the smoke that thunders).

Egypt

Egypt is the most populous country in the Arab world and the third most populated country in Africa.Egypt is a country in North Africa that borders the Red Sea and the Mediterranean Sea.It is famous for its pyramids,pharaohs,mummies,and tombs,flourished for thousands of years.

Nigeria

Nigeria is the most populous black nation on Earth. It is home to the second largest film industry on the globe whilst also being the fashion, technological and creative hub in Africa. Nigerians are known for their vibrant and friendly energy expressed through diverse creative expressions.

Morocco

The Kingdom of Morocco is the most westerly of the North African countries known as the Maghreb - "the West". The cultural richness of Morocco is one of its most attractive features. With influences from Arab, Berber, European, and African civilizations, Moroccan culture offers an incredibly diverse mix that can be observed in its language, architecture, music, and traditions.

Seychelles

Seychelles is a cluster of charming islands found in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, off East Africa.this island nation also proves that it's much more than that with its gorgeous landscapes, abundant nature, and rich culture.

Madagascar

Madagascar, officially the Republic of Madagascar, is an island country comprising the island of Madagascar and numerous smaller peripheral islands. Lying off the southeastern coast of Africa, it is the world's fourth largest island, the second-largest island country and the 46th largest country in the world.
