  1. Playa Manuel Antonio

    Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica. With equal efforts dedicated to wildlife preservation and a beach-getaway atmosphere, Manuel Antonio has surely earned its reputation as one of Costa Rica's most gorgeous locales. A lively resort town sits just north of a national park, where a rain forest teeming with monkeys resides.

  2. La Fortuna Waterfall

    The Fortuna River waterfall is located in a biological reserve with an area of 210 acres of transition premontane tropical wet forest, part of the Arenal Volcano National Park and is located at 520 meters over the sea level, where there are sources of our mountains and the waters of the Fortuna River.

  3. Ecotermales Fortuna

    Ecotermales Fortuna is a serene hot springs resort located in the heart of Arenal, Costa Rica. Nestled amidst lush tropical foliage, this family-owned retreat prides itself on providing a tranquil and intimate environment for guests. With its natural thermal pools heated by the Arenal Volcano's subterranean activity, visitors can enjoy a relaxing soak surrounded by the exquisite beauty of the rainforest.

  4. Rio Celeste

    The Rio Celeste Waterfall is known for its unbelievably blue waters and is quickly becoming one of the most sought-after sights in Costa Rica. The famous blue river and accompanying waterfall is found in the Tenorio Volcano National Park, just outside of Bijagua.

  5. Cahuita National Park

    Cahuita is a unique national park in Costa Rica because of its close relationship with the community. It's an example of shared management for sustainable development between the community and government. The participation of park rangers and community lifeguards stands out through shared governance and management.

  6. Proyecto Asis

    Proyecto Asis is a Wildlife Shelter and Environmental Education Center located very close to La Fortuna. Wild animals which have been found wounded, forsaken or are rescued from the black market by the local forest rangers are brought here. Those that cannot be released are used within our educational programs.
